Our Solutions

MedTech offers a multitude of solutions for any type of provider:

Video Chat for Appointments

The MedTech software platform includes an easy-to-use video conferencing software to set up and attend appointments with patients. Just send a link to your patient to join the virtual meeting and start!

Keep Track of Your Patients

Our intuitive software platform for appointments also has features that keep track of patients and allow users to take e-notes that link with the patient. The software also supports recording meetings with explicit consent from both parties for future reference.

Incorporate Medical Tools into Daily Care

We provide extra medical tools to collect and transmit health data directly from patient to doctor, including blood pressure cuffs, endoscopes and more. All these tools directly connect to the computer via USB and directly send data from patient to doctor without any hassle.

Always Have a Backup with our Tech Support

Customers have access to 24/7 tech support to assist them and guide them through the platform whenever necessary.
